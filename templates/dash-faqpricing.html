Pricing & Payments FAQs

1.  How does Seventh Square charge for the platform and services?

Seventh Square charges the following:

  1. Marketplace commission (only after sale)
  2. Shipping Fee (only if shipping with Seventh Square)
  3. Payment Collection fee (only if payment processed through payment gateway/ cash on delivery)

2.  How much ‘Marketplace Commission’ is charged by Seventh Square?

Seventh Square charges flat 5% across all categories. This is among the lowest across all platforms

3.  How do I receive Payments?

You receive your payments in the Bank Account you register with us.

4.  Where do I check my pending transactions?

Check for any upcoming payment/transfers & past transfers in ‘Payment History’ in your Seller Dashboard menu

4.  When do I receive payments for Orders fulfilled?

Payments to sellers are transferred weekly for all cleared orders. Please refer to Payment Policy for detailed info.

4.  What is the payment process at Seventh Square?

Payments received by the customer through various mediums (Card, Wallets, Cash, Net Banking) are collected with Seventh Square. These are then transferred to your bank account registered with us after charging relevant fees.

4.  How do I price my product to receive my desired amount?

While entering Sale Price during Adding a product, please check ‘See the breakdown’ to see how much you’re receiving on a given sale price level. To find the Sale Price at which you’ll get the desired amount, please check our price calculator.

4.  Can I charge different prices for different quantities?

Yes. For any buyer enquiry for a certain quantity, you can quote a different price than already on the website. You can also mention your bulk quantity rates while adding your product, if you want to.

4.  Who decides the price at which the consumer buys my product?

As a seller on the marketplace, you decide the price of your products.

4.  How do I best price my products?

If you’re a manufacturer, we advise you to price your products accordingly, as we connect you directly to the consumer so he/she can have the benefit of your original products at your own price. However, pricing is your own sole discretion.
