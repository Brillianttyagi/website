Pricing & Payments FAQs

1.  How will I receive payments for products sold?

You will receive the payments in the bank account you register with us on your seller panel. Please refer to Payment Policy for more information on this.

2.  When do I get payments for orders shipped?

You are eligible for payments as soon as return period is over for a particular order. (Please refer merchant agreement for return period of various categories). Your payment cycle is every Tuesday. So, all payments cleared for disbursement will be transferred to your bank account every Tuesday.

3.  Who decides the price of the products?

As a seller, you decide the price of the product. You can decide both ways with Seventh Square, how much should the customer pay or how much you want to receive. Check out price calculator for an example.

4.  How should I price my products?

When Pricing, keep in mind the applicable marketplace fee. Keeping unreasonably high price will deter customers from buying your products. Products cannot be sold at a price higher than MRP.
