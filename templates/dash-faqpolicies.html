Policies FAQs

1.  What is Seventh Square’s return policy?

By default, returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery and in the same condition. However, not all Building materials can be returned. Please refer to our return policy for details. You can reach us at support@seventhsq.com with any concerns related.

2.  What happens in the event of a fraudulent customer complaints?

All complaints are properly studied and evaluated by our team. Based on clearly defined policies, any action is taken. If the complaint is found without basis, your dues will be remitted as per the timeline.

3.  What steps should I follow so I don’t face any fraudulent issues with my deliveries?

You can follow the below steps, so you don’t face any issues with your orders:

  1. Deliver your products properly packaged.
  2. Keep the buyer updated at every step through your dashboard.
  3. Mention all the facts about your product, including storage, transit, etc. to give the customer a full picture before purchases.
  4. Keep video/photo of the order when loading/delivering.

4.  What policies do I agree to when I register as a seller?

We advocate complete transparency. Here is what regulates our platform:

  1. Marketplace Agreement
  2. Privacy Policy
  3. Shipping & Returns Policy
  4. Payments Policy
  5. Storage Policy
  6. Seller Guidelines
  7. Terms of use
