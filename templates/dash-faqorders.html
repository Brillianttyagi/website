Orders FAQs

1.  How do I know if I’ve received any order?

For any order, we notify you using multiple mediums so you don’t miss out on any revenue :

  1. Email notification
  2. Text notification
  3. Seller Dashboard
  4. ‘Orders’ Page

2.  I’m not receiving order notifications. What do I do?

Please register your issue at support@seventhsq.comwith the relevant details and we’ll resolve the issue as soon as possible.

3.  I ran out of inventory. I won’t be able to fulfil the order. What do I do?

Please cancel the order stating the same. We’ll pass the message and the cancellation to the customer, so they can look for an alternative.

4.  Should I always keep inventory?

Yes. Although we don’t make it mandatory to upload inventory, it is advisable to keep sufficient inventory available for any potential orders. Not having inventory repeatedly might influence your brand image negatively. .

5.   I ran out of inventory but I can fulfil the order after 1/2 days. Can I still follow up with the order?

In cases where delay in minimal or client requirement is not urgent, you can update the order as per estimated delivery dispatch and arrival. This helps us bring in complete transparency for both the sellers and the customers. .

6.   I want to set my own return policy. How do I proceed?

Please reach out to us at to review your return policy. .

7.   Where can I see all my orders?

You can access all your current & past orders in your dashboard under ‘Orders’ menu. .

8.   Where can I see all the enquiries I have received?

You can see all customer enquiries for your products in your dashboard. We also send you an email & text for every enquiry. .

9.   How do I respond to the enquiry?

You can respond within the enquiry window. We have made it very easy for you to understand customer requirements and respond to them on your own terms. .

10.   Will the enquiries be confirmly converted into orders?

No. There’s no surety the enquiries will be converted into orders. That’s up to the buyer. We urge you to provide your best quotations to convert as many enquiries into orders. .

11.   How do you determine what enquiries will I receive?

All enquiries matching your serviced regions and products will be sent to you. There are no privileges on the platform to get the queries first or be the only supplier. .

12.   Can I change my quotation once provided?

No. If the buyer accepts your quotation within the provided time frame, it is converted into a Purchase Order, in other terms, order is accepted by both seller & the buyer. .

13.   How does Blanket Purchase Orders work?

Blanket Purchase Orders are long term standing purchase orders for a particular term or project requirements. This helps you, as a seller, to get long term orders and stabilise your volumes/ sales. Buyer also has Price Benefits over a longer term even in dynamic pricing markets. As a seller, you can accept/ reply to any Blanket PO request that you receive in the dashboard, similar to a RFQ. All terms are mentioned within the same document. .

14.   How does Procurement Services/Tools bring in orders for you?

Any Procurement Tool, including RFQ, RFI, RFP, BPO, etc. when accepted by the buyer, are converted in Purchase Orders, with the terms mentioned. These are converted into invoices as per PO’s terms. Fulfilling by you also follows the PO’s terms. .

15.   My Product orders work on sampling of the product. Is there any product sampling service on Seventh Square?

Yes. We help with sampling of the product with the customers, based on customer enquiries. To know more, please reach out to us at seller@seventhsq.com .
