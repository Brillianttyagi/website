Adding New Product : Serviced Regions

FEB 01, 2021
By - Seventh Square

Serviced Regions Field shows your product to customers only in the selected States & Union Territories.

How does it help you?

Serviced Regions is useful in the following cases:

  1. You are a regional or local player manufacturing bulky products like Cement, Bricks, Steel, Aggregates, etc.
  2. Your Product is suitable for certain regions only.

How to use it?

Input names of all the States & UTs you wish to deliver your product to. Once you start typing, we’ll show you recommendations for the same.

If you’re a national player or wish to deliver your products nationally through our platform, you can either leave it blank or enter ‘PAN India’ .here too.
Please connect with our seller help team in case of any queries.
