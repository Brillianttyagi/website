Importance of Packaging in E-commerce

FEB 03, 2021
By - Seventh Square

Packaging is the first thing the customer sees on receiving the order. Figuring out the best suitable packaging is important for online sellers as it not only builds brand perception but also protects from product or order tampering.

Brand Perception

Brand Value is built on consumer’s shopping experience. Inadequate packaging develops a negative opinion about the brand in the consumer’s mind and deters the customer from purchasing from the same seller again. On the other hand, good packaging helps in brand marketing as customers tell their friends about it and also sometimes share it on social media. Even though you and you competitor might have the same product and pricing, order packaging can help you get ahead.

Safety

Any product has to go through various bumps in the road and multiple weather conditions before reaching the customer. Adequate packaging is important here to keep up the product conditions across shipping process by avoiding any product damage or order tampering.

Thus It’s important to develop a good packaging standard suitable to your category. It’s advisable to do so in the starting so your costs are not increased and your business can run as uninterrupted.

So, finally, how to choose suitable packaging?

There are two basic types of packaging – external and internal.

External Packaging involves parcels and flyer bags. Parcels include corrugated boxes, Double or triple walled boxes. These are used to pack heavy items such as electronic appliances, furniture and also fragile items like glass bottles, Sanitaryware etc. A Flyer bag can be used to store small items like boxes, Home Décor etc. They can accommodate products up to 4kg in weight.

Internal packaging materials include bubble wrap, air bags, cardboard, and foam pellets. These have various functions like cushioning, void filling, protection and dividers, and shock absorption. When packing fragile/special products, it is a must that these products be used in ample quantity along with the correct internal packaging to avoid any tampering.

At the end, choose the packaging material, box size, proper seal & labels, and you’ll be on your way to expand your business in no time.
