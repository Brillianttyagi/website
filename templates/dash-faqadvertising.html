Advertising FAQs

1.  What is Seventh Square Advertising?

Seventh Square offers Advertising & Promotional options for sellers who want market their brand as well as their products. This helps them get better visibility on the platform and increases their sales.

2.  What advertising options does Seventh Square have?

Seventh Square offers 3 options to sellers to advertise their product and brand to visitors:

  • Sponsored Products
  • Sponsored Brands
  • Brand Pages

3.  What is Sponsored Products?

Increase your product’s visibility to visitors by ranking on top of search results as customers search for a specific keyword. This helps you gain traction among the customers and helps build sales as well as brand value.

4.  What is Sponsored Brands?

Let customers know you’re the premier in your category by showcasing your brand on the front page as well as relevant product pages with our Sponsored Brands. This helps you increase your brand value as customers see your banner while searching for Home Products and associate your name with the category.

5.  What is Brand Pages?

If you have a good portfolio, you might want to create a separate store for your brand on Seventh Square. Customers will see option to visit your store on Seventh Square many times while searching for keywords relevant to you.

6.  How much does it cost to advertise on Seventh Square?

Advertising spend is relevant when compared to change in sales. So, we have kept most of it variable depending on various factors. Please reach out on seller@seventhsq.com for detailed information as per your requirements.

7.  I still have some doubts. How do I address them?

You can reach out to Seventh Square’s Seller Help at seller@seventhsq.com or write to us here.
