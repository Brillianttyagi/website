Registration FAQs

1.  How do I register as a seller on Seventh Square?

To register as a seller, please go to seller.seventhsq.com and fill in the registration form.

2.  What details are required to become a seller on Seventh Square?

  1. GSTIN
  2. Bank Account
  3. Phone / Email

3.  Can I create an account without GSTIN?

Yes. Your account will be created after the first step, i.e. with Number, Email & Password. However, to successfully start selling on the platform, you have to share your GSTIN and related info.

4.  Where is my GSTIN used?

Your GSTIN is used for :

  1. Identification and verification with us.
  2. Order Invoicing (all invoices are made directly with customers, we act an an intermediary)

5.  Is it safe to share my business info on the platform?

Yes. The website is secure for any personal information you share with us. Also, we do not share your information with any third-party without prior consent.

6.  What do I do if I have an issue with the Seller Agreement during registration?

Although we have drafted our policies keeping in mind the best interests of both sellers and buyers, issues may arise. For any concern regarding agreement, please reach out to support@seventhsq.com .

7.  Are there any Registration or Listing charges on Seventh Square?

No. Registration & Listing is completely free. We only charge in the event of a sale after receiving the amount from the buyer.

8.  Do I need to sign any physical agreement ?

No. Seventh Square uses electronic agreements, under the Information Technology Act, 2000.
