Earning in the 21st century: How e-commerce can help you grow

JAN 25, 2021
By - Seventh Square

I recently heard a story about a woman in a tier-4 city in Rajasthan where she wanted some furniture & furnishings for her house. They got their initial furniture done by a carpenter while they were constructing the house and the new furniture was coming to be too expensive for the carpenter as it was too small an order. She looked within the city, but couldn’t find any readymade furniture worth buying. After exhausting all options, she orders all of it online. It turned out to be better quality within their budget. On getting such a good deal, she was even happy to pay for the expensive furniture shipping which usually deters people from buying in the metros. After this, she started purchasing most of her household items online and is hoping to save enough to remodel her kitchen soon.

This is usually the story of most online shoppers beyond metros. Even in metros, people have experiences of how shopping online has saved them time and money.

Covid-19 effects on retail

The pandemic has given a boost to Home Products being sold online. You must have started hearing people buying their Home décor or furniture for the first time during this pandemic. Even those categories where people preferred to see, feel and then shop are experiencing online traction with the help of many offers from online marketplaces. Furniture & Furnishings, Home Appliances and Home Décor have entered the mature online market category where people are much more comfortable ordering these categories online, while Lighting, Bath & Kitchenware & Home Improvement e-commerce is growing steadily. Before pandemic, around 80 million Indian Households shopped for their Home online, while after Covid-19, it is expected that more than 120 million households will shop in these categories online.

You might have heard digitization and e-commerce is the way to go. But how does it really help you as a brand?

Benefits to brands

Indian consumer is defined by 450 million millennials who prefer convenient, timeless and effortless shopping. Even if you are a retailer in a high end mall or a multi-store retail brand, having an online presence is necessary for growth and building your brand. E-commerce doesn’t just connect you to your customers in your present cities but to consumers across the country. It’s a perfect way to build your brand in a cost effective way and service to customers across the country without having to establish a location or office in every region. Having an online presence also adds to your existing offline presence as it helps in building trust in your brand.

Whether you are a thriving retailer with a brand or a struggling manufacturer, building your online presence and starting with e-commerce will surely help you build and grow your business.
