Shipping & Returns FAQs

1.  Does Seventh Square offer shipping/delivery for orders?

Yes. Seventh Square has a shipping service too. You can choose ‘Shipping with Seventh Square’ while adding a new product if you want to avail shipping services for the product. .

2.   I want to ship my orders myself. How do I choose the relevant settings?

To self ship, you can choose the same while adding a new product. However, to validate self shipping, you have to share your Shipment SLA to help us make the customer experience up to the mark. To find out more about this, please reach out to support@seventhsq.com

3.  I manufacture bulky construction material and shipping is key to my sales. How do I best utilise the platform?

To get guidance regarding your products and shipping, please reach out to us at seller@seventhsq.com .

4.  I have my own logistics network in certain cities/regions. Can I use Seventh Square shipping only where I don’t have my own network?

For customised shipping solutions, please reach out to us at seller@seventhsq.com

5.  I can ship some of my products by myself. Is it possible?

Yes. For each product, you can choose whether you’d like to ship it yourself or with Seventh Square.

6.  I have multiple delivery & storage hubs for my products. How do I proceed with shipping?

In this instance, please mention all your delivery hubs and specific regions serviced through those, while adding your product. If you face any issue, please reach out to us at seller@seventhsq.com

6.  What is a Shipment SLA?

Shipment SLA is a Service Level Agreement detailing your logistics & delivery operations. This will help us display accurate delivery time and in tracking orders as and when placed by the customer.

6.  The customer returned a different product than what was delivered. What do I do?

Please raise a complaint through your dashboard or write to us at seller@seventhsq.com. The issue will be resolved as soon as possible.

6.  My products are bulky / fragile and Customer returns will be too costly for me. Is there any solution to this?

Yes. For Bulky/Fragile or generally unreturnable products, you can select ‘No returns’ while adding your products. This will be shown on your product’s page on www.seventhsq.com to the visitors, so they are completely informed before purchasing any product.

6.  Can the buyer partially return the shipment?

No. Either they can return it completely (only on returnable products) or accept the delivery.

6.  If my shipment is returned, do I have to bear any costs?

No. Except any payment gateway charges (only in online payments by the buyer) or Shipping (if carried out with Seventh Square), no commission or any other charges are incurred to you.
