General FAQs

1.  What products can be sold on Seventh Square?

Any product in the Building Materials & Home Interiors segment can be sold on Seventh Square. Sell Cement, Bricks, Steel, Aggregates, Wood, Glass, Pipes & Fittings, Sanitaryware, Electricals, Roofing, Hardware, Modular & Flooring Products on Seventh Square.

2.  What is a specialised marketplace?

A specialised marketplace is a marketplace platform serving a special consumer segment. Seventh Square is a specialised marketplace platform for Building Materials & Home Interiors.

3.  Why should I sell on Seventh Square?

Seventh Square offers you a dedicated platform to sell your products and build your brand at a time when E-commerce share in the market is increasing quickly. Check out what our specialties are and what additional services we offer to our customers here.

4.  Will customers see my brand / company info while on my product page?

Yes. Your brand along with your brand description will be shown whenever a visitor clicks on your products.

5.  I have certain doubts regarding the policies. What should I do?

For any query related to the platform, please drop us your message at team@seventhsq.com .

6.  How does Seventh Square work?

As an E-commerce Marketplace, Seventh Square offers you to be part of a one-stop Material Procurement platform accessible Pan India. We help you showcase your products to the consumers, offer services in purchasing, delivering & after-sales. Seventh Square is a hub for Building Material Procurement with services & product range not available anywhere else.

7.  What Procurement tools are available on Seventh Square?

Seventh Square has multiple procurement tools available to the Customers, such as, Request for Quotation / Proposal / Information, Blanket Purchase Order, Order Scheduling & Design Room.

8.  I do not want to showcase my products on the website but I am interested in fulfilling the orders. Is it possible?

Seventh Square offers sellers to be offline partners, who register with us. You will receive all orders / enquiries in your segment and you will be able to access all of Seventh Squareâ€™s services but your products would not be shown to the customers on the website.

9.  Who are offline partners? How is it different from regular sellers?

Offline Partners are sellers who do not display their products on the platform, www.seventhsq.com . So, the customers can only purchase these products through enquiries or talking to their relationship managers. Offline partners can still access all the services offered to our regular sellers.
