What is RFx ? How does it help me?

FEB 01, 2021
By - Seventh Square

RFxs are business communications under the family of ‘Request for’. These are used in procurement and help in efficient transactions.

At Seventh Square, to make procurement for buyers much more efficient & organised, we have included several RFxs :

Request for Quotation

Request for Quotation is used when you want to get price quotes for a fixed quantity from a certain set of suppliers. This is also helpful in cases of customized products like PEB, Automation, Flooring, etc. As sellers, you can state your pricing for a certain quantity with detailed specifications that the buyer wants to buy .

Request for Proposal

When buyers require a certain service and/or product and are open to exploring offers from several different vendors, RFP is an ideal option. An RFP helps buyers get offers from several different vendors who are willing to service their requirements with specified details about the products, services & price offered. As a seller, you can respond to the RFP in the dashboard itself.

Request for Information

An RFI is purely informational and is used to obtain information about a certain product, vendor or a particular requirement. This is very helpful as buyers are much more interested once they have complete information as per their requirements. Answer as many RFIs as you can in the dashboard.

Blanket Purchase Orders

Blanket POs help bigger projects streamline their procurement. When buyers are working on a large project, the project timeline is for years. Many products have dynamic pricing in the building materials market. Thus, Blanket PO helps them procure building materials in multiple orders for the same price and other specifications set in the PO. As a seller, this helps you in securing large orders with legally binding purchase orders

To get help with responding to RFx and Blanket PO, please reach out to us at seller@seventhsq.com
