How to write Product Title and Product Description that increase sales

JAN 25, 2021
By - Seventh Square

In E-Commerce, your basic product information is the first touch point in sales. It determines if the customer will proceed to know more about your product or move on to the next option. This is why portraying your product right on online platforms can be extremely important to your sales growth.
Use this article to gain the copywriting advantage in Online Selling and increase your audience and sales with us.

Product Title is what helps your customers discover you, judge the product and finalise the sale to an extent. It’s that easy and important. As per research, good product titles help you increase sales by 70-80% while inefficient product titles can reduce your sales by more than 20%. In order to write an effective product title:

  • Use language that your target consumer might use for search

    For example, in case of Construction Chemicals, instead of using ‘Branded Sealoxy Primer’, it might help to use ‘Arcylic Primer for External and Internal Walls’ . This helps not only in Keywords discovery but also helps the consumer understand the product.

  • Easy to Follow format

    We have provided below a format that is easy to follow for your customers as well as you and will help you in communicating exactly what the product is and why the customer should buy it : Brand Name + Product Type + Size/Quantity + Relevant Keywords (if any) For Example, Ultratech OPC 53 Cement 50kgs Bag
    Astral UPVC 1” Pipe 100mm Length

Product Description helps visitors know about the product without visiting the shop or talking to a salesman. This is what takes the sale to the finish line. A consumer friendly description is both easy to write and extremely helpful in communicating the key benefits of your product. Here’s what you can include in your Product Description to make it more attractive to your customer:

  • Include Key Features of your Product

    Why should the consumer buy the product from you? What makes it different from other competitors? What are the key attributes of the products?

  • Include Benefits and Applications for more understanding

    How purchasing your product would help the customer? Where can the product be used or applied? When should the product be used?

  • Keywords

    This helps in ranking and discoverability of the product for the consumer. If you have any key points or words that make up your product, it’d be helpful to mention those in the Description box.
