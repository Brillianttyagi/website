Part B

Payment Collection Fee

  • Sellers will be charged for using payment gateway and other payment collection methods like Cash-onDelivery.
  • Seventh Square ensures maximum availability of payment methods to customers/users of the platform and a simpler remit to the sellers.
  • A fee of 3% on selling price will be charged as Payment collection fees.
  • This payment collection fees is for using the online payment gateway and/or for cash collection by courier partners.

Part C

Shipping Fees

  • The following shipping fees will be deducted from the selling price.
  • Shipping fees can be adjusted within the selling price or shown differently to the buyers as delivery fee (completely or partially).
  • Shipping fees is based on Product weight (Dead weight or Volumetric) and Shipping Destination.
  • However, Seventh Square has simplified the shipping price for sellers as well as buyers.
  • Volumetric weight will be calculated as : Length*Breadth*Height (in cms) / 4000 . The resulting number would be considered in Kgs.
  • Higher or the Dead Weight or Volumetric Weight would be considered for shipping cost.
  • If, by mistake, the seller enters the wrong weight (as per guidelines), the courier partner will update it and if any gain/loss would be borne by the seller.
  • The rate card is exclusive of Taxes i.e. 18% GST. Input tax credit can be claimed by the sellers on the same.
