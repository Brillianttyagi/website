Tips & Tricks For Selling Online

JAN 25, 2021
By - Seventh Square

Establishing your brand online might seem a hassle from far but it doesn’t have to be so difficult creating your name among marketplaces as compared to when you started with a small retail space.

Here is some expert advice to make your online selling journey easier with us:

Detailed Product Listing

Keep your product listing fully updated. Put any specific information that the customer might want to know in description and explain how to use your product. You only have to do it once and thousands of consumers will see it when they open your product page. This helps consumers be comfortable purchasing your product from the website. Also, this helps in ranking your products based on keywords and website searches. So, with a detailed and optimized listing, your products rank first as well as are more likely to be sold. Here are some actions for completing your listing:
  • Optimize your title
  • Relevant Dimensions
  • Material
  • Use
  • Care tips
  • Warranty or Guarantee if any
  • Brand info
  • Keywords

Product Photography

The first thing a consumer notices while buying a product online is how it looks. Even if you have a great product, poor quality photographs may end up losing customer’s interest in your product. Having professional photographs will pay off greatly by increasing your sales as well as brand image. Pricing Strategy

You might see people selling online at exorbitant prices, but that shouldn’t be the case. The customer expects the same price range for offline and online, which results in many sellers losing out on potential sales due to high pricing. See your online commerce as another store but digitally. Figure out your costs, which could include warehousing, packaging and marketing and set your margins. Mostly you’ll get your online store’s price to be lower as you save on physical store’s rent and other expenses as well as lower costs of digital marketing.

You can set your pricing to be stable or competitive as per your business objectives.

Customer Feedback

Does the customer give feedback at your retail space regarding store services and product quality? This is the same. It’s extremely valuable here though as their feedback is visible publicly. Thus, good customer feedback not only improves your sales but also your brand value as it builds trust in your brand and also improves your product ranking in search pages.
